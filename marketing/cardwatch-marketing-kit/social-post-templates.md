## Usage Guidelines

1. **Always use real examples** — Swap in actual card names, prices, and screenshots from your CardWatch alerts
2. **Don't post the same template twice in the same community** — Rotate formats
3. **Reddit: Don't over-promote** — The deal-find and question posts work best because they provide value first. Limit explicit CardWatch mentions to 1 in 3-4 posts
4. **Twitter/X: Use screenshots** — Visual tweets perform 2-3x better than text-only
5. **Facebook: Lead with value** — Groups moderate heavily. Share a deal or ask a question; don't just post an ad
6. **Track which templates get engagement** — Double down on what works, retire what doesn't
