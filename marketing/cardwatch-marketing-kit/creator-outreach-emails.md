# Creator Outreach Email Sequence

Guidelines: Write as a peer, not a vendor. No corporate jargon. Single, low-friction CTA per email. Subject lines: 2-4 words, lowercase, no punctuation.

---

## Email 1: First Touch

**Subject:** your [player name] video

**Body:**

Hey [First Name],

Watched your video on [specific video title or topic] — the part about [specific detail from their content] was spot on.

I built a tool called CardWatch that scans eBay every 15 minutes and sends alerts when specific cards get listed. Basically catches underpriced listings before most people see them.

I think your audience would genuinely get value from it — especially the flippers and deal hunters.

I'm offering creators 100% of the first month's subscription for every person they refer, plus 20% ongoing. You'd also get a free lifetime account.

Worth a 10-minute call this week to see if it's a fit?

Pierce
CardWatch — mycardwatch.com

---

## Email 2: Follow-Up (3-4 days later)

**Subject:** quick follow up

**Body:**

Hey [First Name],

Following up on my last note about CardWatch.

Here's what it looks like in practice: last week the tool caught a [specific card example] listed at $45 — comps were at $180. That's the kind of alert your audience would get.

I put together a one-pager with the creator deal details: [link to one-pager or attach]

If the timing's off right now, no worries — just let me know and I'll circle back later.

Pierce

---

## Email 3: Breakup Email (5-7 days after Email 2)

**Subject:** closing the loop

**Body:**

Hey [First Name],

Don't want to clog your inbox, so this is my last note on this.

If CardWatch ever makes sense for your audience — eBay alerts, deal catching, the whole thing — the creator program will still be here. You'd keep 100% of the first month per referral plus 20% ongoing.

Either way, keep making great content. That [specific recent video/post] was solid.

Pierce
mycardwatch.com/partners

---

## Outreach Checklist Before Sending

- [ ] Watched at least one of their recent videos/posts
- [ ] Noted a specific detail to reference (not generic flattery)
- [ ] Confirmed they're in the sports card niche (not general collectibles/toys)
- [ ] Checked their follower count (target 5k-50k for nano/micro)
- [ ] Personalized the [bracketed] sections
- [ ] Verified their email or DM preference (some prefer Instagram/Twitter DMs)

## Where to Find Creator Contact Info

1. YouTube "About" tab — many list business email
2. Instagram/TikTok bio — often has email or Linktree
3. Twitter/X bio
4. If no public email, DM on the platform where they're most active
5. Use Hunter.io or similar if they have a website
